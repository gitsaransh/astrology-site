Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 5, 2026

At Jyotish Paramarsh, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you visit our website or book a consultation.

1. Information We Collect

When you book a consultation, we may collect the following personal information:

  • Name
  • Phone Number (for WhatsApp communication)
  • Date of Birth, Time of Birth, and Place of Birth (strictly for astrological calculations)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely for the following purposes:

  • To prepare your detailed birth chart (Kundli).
  • To conduct the consultation session.
  • To communicate with you regarding your appointment or billing.

We do NOT sell, trade, or rent your personal identification information to others.

3. Data Protection

Your birth details and personal concerns discussed during the consultation are kept strictly confidential. We implement appropriate data collection, storage, and processing practices to protect against unauthorized access.

4. Third-Party Tools

We use trusted third-party services like WhatsApp for communication and Calendly/Google/Zoom for scheduling. Please review their respective privacy policies if you have concerns.
